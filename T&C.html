Terms & Conditions

Exchange

Products are liable to exchange only if they are damaged or incorrect product has been delivered. In either of these cases we will resolve the issue to your satisfaction. You will need to inform us of any damages / incorrect delivery within 48 hours of delivery of the product, in order to receive the replacement.
Please mail us at help@plumms.com or call us on 080-41128219 to initiate an exchange. Please mention the following information clearly in the mail:

  • Order Number
  • Short description of the condition of the product at the time of delivery.
  • Pick-Up Address and pincode

Replacement will be shipped after the original item has been received in the warehouse, subject to the following conditions:

  • The item/s should be unused, unsoiled and should not be brought in contact with water.
  • The packaging should have the original tags in place.

Any returned item received by us that does not meet the above mentioned conditions will not be accepted. No amount will be reimbursed in this case.
